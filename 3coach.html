Rotation Strategy

Rotate Smart. Survive Longer.

Rotation is the most underrated skill in Free Fire. Know when to push, when to rotate, and how to always have an escape plan.

PHASE 1 — DROP
Landing Phase
  • Land at edge zones first match
  • Hot drops only if confident in gunfights
  • Spread out but stay within 100m of each other
  • Priority: weapons → ammo → armour → medkits
PHASE 2 — MID GAME
Zone Rotation
  • Start rotating when 2nd zone appears
  • Always move with cover — trees, rocks, buildings
  • Scout uses Gloo Wall to create paths
  • IGL calls direction — team follows immediately
PHASE 3 — END GAME
Final Circle
  • Take high ground before zone closes
  • Healer stays center of squad formation
  • Rusher waits for IGL call before engaging
  • Use Gloo Walls to force enemy position
🚗 Vehicle Escape Routes
  • Always note vehicle spawns before engaging a fight — know your exit
  • Jeep can carry 4 players — ideal for full squad rotation
  • Motorbike is fastest — use for solo scout rotations
  • Never drive in straight lines — zigzag to avoid sniper fire
  • Drop vehicles 50m before your destination to avoid giving position
🚀 Launchpad Tactics
  • Use launchpads to surprise enemies from unexpected angles
  • Pre-place Gloo Walls at landing spot before jumping
  • IGL calls launchpad rotation — team goes one at a time
  • Healer jumps last to cover squad landing
  • Never use launchpad when fully in the open — enemies will track you

👑 IGL (In-Game Leader) Guide
  • IGL always calls — team never acts without the call unless emergency
  • Before every match: agree on drop location, roles, and rotation plan
  • IGL watches the minimap while others focus on combat
  • Good IGL = calm voice, clear calls, adapts instantly to changing situation
  • IGL does NOT need to be the best gunfighter — best decision-maker wins
  • End-game: IGL tells each player which target to focus — no confusion
⚔️ Squad Formation
  • Scout moves 20m ahead — spots enemies, calls info back
  • Rusher flanks left or right on IGL command
  • Assaulter takes center — highest DPS in the fight
  • Healer stays 15m behind — never front line, always cover
  • Keep 10–15m spacing — avoid grenade kills wiping squad
  • After a kill: hold position, wait for IGL call before advancing
